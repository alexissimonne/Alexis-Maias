HELLO!

If you’re here reading this, you want to get to know me a little bit better and I appreciate that. The name is Alexis and I am a Bay Area native. I was born in San Jose and have lived in Fremont since I was eight. I am currently working towards my BA in Graphic Design and a minor in Photography at San Jose State University. I aspire to become a presentation designer while still being able to enjoy my love for photography. When I’m not creating or shooting, I’m at work as an AR clerk for a car dealership(temporarily) or I’m out adventuring with my eight year old son, Jadyn. I have had quite the journey in getting to where I am now and cannot wait for this chapter of my life to evolve.

Hope you enjoy! XO
